📘 IBBUL Guide App

A campus app for academics and student life.

About This Project

IBBUL Guide is a student-focused app with GPA/CGPA calculators, campus map, faculty info, admission tips, and real-time school news.

Key Features

  • 📊 GPA/CGPA calculator
  • 🗺️ Campus map with navigation
  • 📂 Faculty & department info
  • 📡 News feed

Technologies

Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, DataStore, Google Maps SDK
